ABOUT US

The UST Salinggawi Dance Troupe, the official dance troupe of the University of Santo Tomas, stands as one of the premier cultural organizations in the university. Founded in 1970 under the name "UST Folk Dance Troupe," the troupe embodies her commitment to preserving and promoting Filipino cultural heritage. With a background as an accomplished performing group, the troupe has consistently showcased exceptional performances and has evolved to become one of the most reputable and contemporary expressions of Filipino culture.

The name Salinggawi derived from the Filipino phrase "Saling-gawi ng Lahi" which means "merging of cultures." This name aptly reflects the troupe's mission to bridge the past and the present through dance. Known for its versatility, the troupe performs a wide range of dance genres including traditional Filipino dances, contemporary, street dancing, modern fusion, jazz/hip-hop, cheerleading, and production numbers. The UST Salinggawi Dance Troupe continues to uphold excellence while preserving the rich cultural legacy of our nation.

Mission

The UST SDT are recognized dance troupe of the University known for its versatility in performing different genres of dance, are founded to be consistent with their hard work, artistry, and creativity, and to continuously aim high level and culture of excellence in promoting Filipino culture through dance. The troupe aims for the integration of the growth in individual discipline, resilient, learning, integrity, having a passion through the passion training.

Vision

The UST SDT envisions to be a globally competitive dance troupe by achieving perfection through discipline towards the attainment of excellence. The troupe aims to be a Cultural Icon that will set trends, promote dance art as a Cultural asset that embodies Filipino values and traditions. The troupe aspires transformation to having responsible leaders who will enrich and propagate the nation's dance and cultural heritage through dance.
